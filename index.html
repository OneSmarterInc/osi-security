How Krypteon Uses Your Personal Information

This policy covers how Krypteon treats personal information that Krypteon collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Krypteon products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.

This privacy policy only applies to Krypteon.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Krypteon does not own or control, or to people that Krypteon does not employ or manage.



Information Collection and Use

General

Krypteon collects personal and/or business information when you register with Krypteon, when you use Krypteon products or services, when you visit Krypteon pages and/or the pages of certain Krypteon partners. Krypteon may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other affiliated companies.



Information Sharing and Disclosure

Krypteon does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Krypteon under confidentiality agreements.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Krypteon's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if Krypteon is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Krypteon will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.


Cookies

Krypteon may set and access Krypteon cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Krypteon uses web beacons to access Krypteon cookies inside and outside our network of web sites and in connection with Krypteon products and services.
